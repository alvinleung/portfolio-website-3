## Celebrate the Speaker

Through interviewing stakeholders, we found that speakers and their topics are the major motivating factor for the audience to attend. With that in mind, the content approach prioritises surfacing the respective information elegantly in order to entice potential the tedx attendance.

## Testing & Interation

Before development, we ran a formative user research session on the prototype with 6 SFU students. We also asked a middle aged parent with visual impairment to participate with the intent of stress testing the system usability.
